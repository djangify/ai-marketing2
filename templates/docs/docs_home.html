FAQ

What file types can I upload?

AI Marketing Platform supports text (.txt, .md), document (.pdf), audio (.mp3, .wav, .m4a, .ogg), and video (.mp4, .mov, .avi, .wmv) files.

What are tokens and how are they calculated?

Tokens are units of text that the AI processes. A token is roughly 4 characters or 3/4 of a word. Your subscription includes a specific token allowance for both assets and prompts.

Can I export content in different formats?

Yes! You can export generated content as plain text (.txt), Markdown (.md), or Microsoft Word (.docx) documents.

How do I increase my token allowance?

You can upgrade your subscription plan from your account settings to increase your token allowance.

Is my content secure?

Yes. All your uploads and generated content are securely stored and only accessible to your account. We do not use your content to train our AI models.
