1. Getting Started

Welcome to the AI Marketing Platform! This quick start documentation will give you a brief outline of how to use our platform to create AI-generated marketing content. For more detailed help use the documentation available. The link is in your dashboard.

Quick Start Guide

The basic workflow consists of:

  1. Create a project
  2. Upload assets (text, audio, or video files)
  3. Create prompts or use templates
  4. Generate content
  5. Edit and export your content

2. Projects

Projects help you organize your content assets and AI-generated marketing materials.

2.1 Creating a Project

To create a new project:

  1. Go to your Dashboard
  2. Click on "Create New Project" under Quick Actions
  3. Enter a title for your project
  4. Add optional keywords to guide AI content generation
  5. Click "Create Project"

2.2 Managing Projects

You can view all your projects from the Projects page. From there, you can:

  • Edit project details
  • Delete projects
  • Filter projects by tags

3. Assets

Assets are the content files you upload to your projects. The AI analyzes these assets to generate marketing content.

3.1 Supported File Types

  • Text files (.txt)
  • Markdown files (.md)
  • PDF documents (.pdf)
  • Audio files (MP3, WAV, etc.)
  • Video files (MP4, etc.)

3.2 Uploading Assets

To upload assets to a project:

  1. Open your project
  2. Click on the "Upload" tab
  3. Drag and drop files or click to select files
  4. Click "Upload Files"

4. Prompts

Prompts guide the AI in generating content. Well-crafted prompts lead to better content generation results. We have provided additional training on prompt marketing in our documentation

4.1 Creating Prompts

To create a prompt:

  1. Open your project
  2. Click on the "Prompts" tab
  3. Click "Add Prompt"
  4. Enter a name and the prompt text
  5. Click "Save"

4.2 Prompt Writing Tips

  • Be specific about the type of content you want
  • Include target audience information
  • Specify tone (casual, professional, etc.)
  • Mention desired length and format
  • Include keywords you want to emphasize

5. Templates

Templates are pre-configured sets of prompts for common marketing needs. They save time and provide consistent results.

5.1 Using Templates

To use a template in your project:

  1. Open your project
  2. Click on the "Prompts" tab
  3. Click "Load Template"
  4. Select a template from the list
  5. Click "Import"

5.2 Creating Your Own Templates

To create a new template:

  1. Go to the Templates page
  2. Click "New Template"
  3. Enter a title and description
  4. Add prompts to your template
  5. Click "Save"

6. Content Generation

Once you've added assets and prompts, you can generate content with our AI.

6.1 Generating Content

To generate content:

  1. Open your project
  2. Click on the "Generate" tab
  3. Click "Generate Content"
  4. Wait for the generation process to complete

6.2 Editing Generated Content

After content is generated, you can:

  • Edit the content directly
  • Regenerate specific sections
  • Copy content to your clipboard
  • Save content for later use

7. Frequently Asked Questions

7.1 How many projects can I create?

You can create unlimited projects with your subscription.

7.2 What happens to my assets?

Your assets are securely stored and only used to generate content for your projects. We do not share or use your data for training our AI.

7.3 Can I export the generated content?

Yes, you can copy the generated content to your clipboard and use it in any way you need.

7.4 How does the token system work?

Tokens measure the amount of text processed by the AI. Your subscription includes a certain number of tokens for assets and prompts.

7.5 Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.
