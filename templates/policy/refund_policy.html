Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Contents:

1. Introduction

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions for subscription cancellations and refunds for AI Marketing Platform services. By subscribing to our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

This policy should be read in conjunction with our Terms & Conditions.

2. Subscription Plans

AI Marketing Platform offers various subscription plans with different pricing, features and/or limitations. The details of each plan are described on our website at the time of purchase.

2.1 Billing Cycle

Subscriptions are billed on a recurring basis (monthly, quarterly, or yearly) depending on the plan selected. The billing cycle begins on the date of the initial subscription purchase and continues until the subscription is cancelled.

2.2 Automatic Renewal

All subscriptions automatically renew at the end of the billing period unless cancelled prior to the renewal date. By subscribing to our services, you authorize us to charge your payment method for the subscription fee at the beginning of each billing period.

3. Cancellation Policy

3.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time through:

  • Your account dashboard under "Subscription Settings"
  • Contacting our customer support team at admin@aimarketingplatform.app

3.2 Cancellation Effects

When you cancel your subscription:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. For example, if you are on a monthly plan and cancel on the 15th day of the month, your subscription will remain active until the end of that monthly period.
  • You will continue to have full access to all subscription features until the end of the current billing period.
  • Your subscription will not renew for the next billing period.
  • No partial refunds will be issued for the unused portion of your current billing period.

3.3 Data Retention After Cancellation

After your subscription ends:

  • You will no longer have access to create new content or use our AI services.
  • Your account data and previously generated content will be retained for 30 days after the end of your subscription period.
  • To permanently delete your account and all associated data, please contact our support team.

4. Refund Policy

Important: AI Marketing Platform does not offer refunds for subscription payments. All subscription purchases are final.

4.1 No-Refund Policy

We do not provide refunds for subscription fees under any circumstances, including but not limited to:

  • Accidental purchases
  • Dissatisfaction with the service
  • Unused subscription time
  • Technical issues that are resolved during the subscription period
  • Changes in pricing or features

4.2 Exception for Service Unavailability

In the rare event of extended service unavailability (more than 72 consecutive hours) due to our technical issues, we may, at our sole discretion, offer compensation in the form of:

  • Extended subscription time
  • Credit towards future subscription periods

Such compensation is not guaranteed and will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

5. Payment Disputes

If you believe you have been incorrectly charged, please contact our support team before initiating a chargeback or payment dispute with your payment provider. Most billing issues can be quickly resolved by contacting us directly.

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute without first attempting to resolve the issue with us may result in:

  • Immediate termination of your subscription
  • Suspension of your account
  • Ineligibility for future subscriptions

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

We will notify users of any material changes to this policy via email or through a notification on our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund & Cancellation Policy, please contact us at:

Email: admin@aimarketingplatform.app
