1. Introduction

This Advertising Policy outlines the guidelines and standards for content generated using the AI Marketing Platform. Our platform is designed to help users create effective marketing content while adhering to ethical advertising practices and applicable regulations.

While our AI tools can help you generate creative and compelling marketing content, users are ultimately responsible for ensuring that all content they create and publish complies with relevant laws, regulations, and platform-specific advertising policies.

2. Content Generation Guidelines

When using our platform to generate marketing content, we encourage users to follow these guidelines:

2.1 Accuracy and Truthfulness

All claims in your marketing content should be accurate, truthful, and substantiated. Avoid generating content that makes false or misleading claims about products, services, or competitive offerings. Remember that AI-generated content may need to be fact-checked before publication.

2.2 Transparency

Be transparent about the nature of your products or services. Do not use AI-generated content to obscure or misrepresent material information that consumers need to make informed decisions.

2.3 Clarity

Content should clearly communicate the offer, including any conditions, limitations, or qualifications. Avoid generating content with ambiguous or confusing messaging that could mislead consumers.

3. Prohibited Content

Our platform should not be used to generate content that:

3.1 Illegal or Harmful Content

  • Promotes illegal products, services, or activities
  • Encourages harmful or dangerous behavior
  • Contains hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory content
  • Infringes on others' intellectual property rights
  • Violates privacy or confidentiality regulations

3.2 Deceptive Practices

    3.2 Deceptive Practices

    • Makes false promises or guarantees
    • Creates fake testimonials or reviews
    • Misrepresents the effectiveness of a product or service
    • Uses bait-and-switch tactics
    • Mimics news articles or government communications without appropriate disclaimers

    3.3 Restricted Categories

    Our platform should not be used to generate marketing content for:

    • Tobacco, vaping, or related products
    • Weapons, ammunition, or explosives
    • Adult/sexually explicit content
    • Gambling or betting services (unless explicitly permitted in your jurisdiction with proper licensing)
    • Counterfeit goods
    • Unauthorized pharmaceutical promotion

4. AI-Generated Content Considerations

4.1 Human Review

All AI-generated content should be reviewed and edited by a human before publication. Our AI systems are designed to assist content creation, not to replace human judgment, especially for factual verification and compliance checks.

4.2 Disclosure of AI Use

In some contexts, it may be appropriate or required to disclose that content was created with AI assistance. Be aware of evolving industry standards and regulations regarding AI content disclosure.

4.3 Limitations of AI

Understand that AI systems have limitations and may:

  • Generate content based on outdated information
  • Create factually incorrect statements
  • Produce content that inadvertently violates copyright or intellectual property rights
  • Miss nuances related to cultural sensitivity or context

Users must independently verify the accuracy and appropriateness of all AI-generated content.

5. Regulatory Compliance

Users are responsible for ensuring their marketing content complies with all applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

5.1 Advertising Regulations

  • Truth in advertising laws
  • Industry-specific advertising regulations (e.g., financial services, healthcare)
  • Environmental claims and sustainability marketing guidelines

5.2 Data Privacy

  • GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
  • Marketing consent requirements
  • Data collection and use disclosures

5.3 Platform-Specific Policies

When creating content for specific platforms (e.g., social media networks, search engines), users must also comply with each platform's advertising policies, which may be more restrictive than general regulations.

6. Best Practices

To maximize the effectiveness and compliance of AI-generated marketing content, we recommend the following best practices:

6.1 Prompt Engineering

Create clear, specific prompts that include any necessary compliance requirements, target audience information, and brand guidelines. Better prompts generally lead to more accurate and compliant content generation.

6.2 Content Verification

Implement a verification process for all AI-generated content, including:

  • Fact-checking all claims and statistics
  • Reviewing for compliance with relevant regulations
  • Checking for brand consistency
  • Verifying that all statements align with product or service capabilities

6.3 Continuous Improvement

Keep a record of successful content and effective prompts. Review and refine your approach to AI-generated marketing content based on performance and compliance considerations.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Advertising Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or best practices in AI-generated content. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Significant changes will be communicated to users through email or platform notifications.
